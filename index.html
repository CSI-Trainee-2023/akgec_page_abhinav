About Akgec


The college was established in 1998
and offers B.Tech Courses in seven
disciplines of Engineering. The college also
offers M.Tech in Automation and Robotics,
Electronics & Communication Engineering,
Computer Science, Electrical and Electronics Engineering
and Mechanical Engineering.
